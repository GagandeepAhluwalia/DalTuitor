About Dal Tutors

Founded in 2018 by Dalhousie alumni, Dal Tutors was created to help students navigate the challenges of university courses with personalized support from peers who have successfully completed the same courses.

What started as a small group of tutors helping fellow students has grown into Halifax's premier tutoring service for Dalhousie students, with over 150 tutors covering 200+ courses across all faculties.

Our Mission

We believe every Dalhousie student deserves access to affordable, high-quality academic support from tutors who truly understand their courses and challenges.

Our mission is to create a supportive learning community where students can get the help they need, when they need it, from peers who have been in their shoes.
